Your MySQL timeout is too low for your server. PVPGN makes one connection to
MySQL, if your timeout is too low, once it loses connection, it does not
reconnect, and thus a reboot of the PVPGN server becomes necessary. Change
your timeout in MySQL to a higher value and you will no longer see this
problem. (Make sure it is a value which is large enough for your server, if
you don't have a large playerbase, don't set the number small, especially if
your users only play during one time of the day.
